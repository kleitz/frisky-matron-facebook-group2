{% raw %}a good deal of information unknown to the outside{% endraw %}
{% raw %}public. Ventnor had been in the States, and had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not been very far from where Oudenard had camped{% endraw %}
{% raw %}when shooting in the Rockies ; that is to say, only{% endraw %}
{% raw %}about six hundred miles awaj'—not far for America.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Lord Oudenard had been in the Rattlesnake{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mountains near the Yellow Stone, when Ventnor{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was at the Marble Canyon, in Colorado. Before{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they separated it was settled that they should lunch{% endraw %}
{% raw %}together next day at the Helix, a club for which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Ventnor had had his name put up on the first anni¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}versary of his birth, and to which, in good times or{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bad times, he had kept up his subscription continu¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ously. First, however, Oudenard was to breakfast{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with Ventnor next morning at the Metropole, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}then they were to go together and look at some horses.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Having done so, they were returning towards the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Metropole, when Ventnor said :{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Do you mind my going in here to look at a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}directory ? I want to find the address of certain{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lawyers named Spangam and Spink.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' I can tell you that without any directory,' said{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Oudenard ; ' they happen to be my own men. Live{% endraw %}
{% raw %}up Chancery Lane—I forget the number, but I know{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the place well. Want to see them ?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Yes,' said Ventnor; ' I have an appointment{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with them to-day either for twelve o'clock or three{% endraw %}
{% raw %}o'clock, as may suit.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Just going twelve now,' said Oudenard. ' Come{% endraw %}
{% raw %}along, I'll show you the way.'{% endraw %}
