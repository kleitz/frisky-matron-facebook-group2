{% raw %}THE PROPOSAL 69{% endraw %}
{% raw %}much can be done. Each day since I first met you{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I have felt that we were becoming better friends. I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}believed that I was fated to get every day nearer and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nearer.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' And never quite to reach,' said she. ' Prince, I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cannot marry you, but I will be an asymptote to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you. Excuse the expression, which I am far from{% endraw %}
{% raw %}using in a frivolous sense. It puts the case as it{% endraw %}
{% raw %}really stands.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He felt that she meant him to understand that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}what he wished for was in the language of science{% endraw %}
{% raw %}absurd and impossible. He was badly hit—very{% endraw %}
{% raw %}badly hit—but it behoved him to bear up like a man.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He offered her his arm and brought her to the door,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and put her into a cab which he hailed. She drove{% endraw %}
{% raw %}away really grieved. She certainly had refused a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}prince, and that was some slight consolation. He{% endraw %}
{% raw %}walked along the Boulevard St. Michel, grinding his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}teeth. He was not more conceited than other men^{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which means that he was only about four times as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vain as an ordinary woman, and his amour propre{% endraw %}
{% raw %}had sustained a deep wound. Because Miss Browne{% endraw %}
{% raw %}liked his society, and showed that she considered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}him an agreeable, cultivated and amusing person,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and a desirable partner to play golf and tennis with,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}he had rashly concluded that she was in love with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}him. And now all was over.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It was not merely his vanity that was hurt: he{% endraw %}
{% raw %}had given his whole soul to this girl, and her in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fluence had made him a purer and better man.{% endraw %}
