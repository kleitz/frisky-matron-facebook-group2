{% raw %}42 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The third and last set of Lancers was about to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}commence as Kitty Browne stood up with Yussuff.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}She continued without an effort the sketch of what{% endraw %}
{% raw %}she thought ought to be altered in the constitution{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Montana, a subject under discussion at the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}moment of his introduction to her. Yussuff ex¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pressed surprise at her minute knowledge of so{% endraw %}
{% raw %}intricate a subject.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Oh,' said she, ' I know all about statesmanship ;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}my Pop always talks politics to me.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Now be it remarked that this was the first and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the last time that he ever heard from Miss Browne{% endraw %}
{% raw %}what English people call an Americanism. Really{% endraw %}
{% raw %}well-bred Americans have no Americanisms and no{% endraw %}
{% raw %}accent. There are three hundred and ninety-two{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thousand people who agree without one dissentient{% endraw %}
{% raw %}voice that the best English is spoken in Boston.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Classical music is an excellent promoter of con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}versation. Next to it comes dancing. Miss Browne{% endraw %}
{% raw %}talked well on every subject. She had been only{% endraw %}
{% raw %}two years in Paris, but she knew it better than many{% endraw %}
{% raw %}natives of that city of light. An American in a week{% endraw %}
{% raw %}will know more of London than the smartest{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cockney of them all. Yussuff felt intensely in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}terested in this fully developed and educated,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}yet most essentially feminine, woman, and he felt a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}momentary irritation at the restraints of convention¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ality which rendered it so impossible on first ac¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quaintance to seek for the inward ideas and real{% endraw %}
{% raw %}feelings of a personality which promised so much.{% endraw %}
