{% raw %}THE CAFE ROCOCO 135{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'Two thousand five hundred francs!' said the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}judge, in long, drawn-out accents of surprise; ' and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you call that playing in a small way ? Monsieur,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the statements you have just made bear an aspect{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of very great seriousness.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Further witnesses were then called, and the events{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the second platform were repeated in great detail.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The commissaire detailed how Alphonse had come{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to him out of breath with running, and informed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}him that a murder had been committed. At this{% endraw %}
{% raw %}word a cold shudder seemed to pass through the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}assembly. He described reaching the first platform{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and finding all the party there save two. Before{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ascending, he had placed a cordon of police round{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the tower at thirty metres interval, and had stationed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a party at the foot of the great spiral staircase. He{% endraw %}
{% raw %}had himself ascended the ladder in the central{% endraw %}
{% raw %}column and found it empty- He had reached the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}summit and found no one there.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The four police who captured Dunne were then{% endraw %}
{% raw %}called. They described his resistance as terrific.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}They spoke of his frantic efforts to reach his hip-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pocket, and stated that he was a man of prodigious{% endraw %}
{% raw %}strength and the fiercest courage, so much so that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for even four men to subdue him was an act of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}heroism. He had fought with all the energy and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}desperation of despair.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Why,' said Dunne, ' in the dark I thought I was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}attacked by robbers. I fought for my life.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A low hum went through the audience.{% endraw %}
