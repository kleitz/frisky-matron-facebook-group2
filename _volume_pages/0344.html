{% raw %}342 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}knew Suez better than I did, as I had been three{% endraw %}
{% raw %}weeks in the Suez police, while waiting for a remit¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tance. I knew that I was in a den of thieves, un¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}surpassed in the world, perhaps unequafled ; but I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}had one grand flutter, and found myself with scarcely{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a sou of ready money.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' That, however, did not trouble me. I had my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}own letter of credit on a French bank, and I could{% endraw %}
{% raw %}easily get an advance on it in Cairo. I sold a ring{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and took train for that city. I went to a bank there{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and explained my difficulty. The manager at once{% endraw %}
{% raw %}agreed to advance me forty pounds, to take me to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Paris. He rang the bell; the cashier came in.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' " Pay Mr. Ventnor," said he, " forty pounds on{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his letter of credit, and notify to Paris." He held{% endraw %}
{% raw %}out his hand. "Your passport," said he, "as a mere{% endraw %}
{% raw %}matter of form."{% endraw %}
{% raw %}* " I have no passport," said I.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'"Ah," said he, "that is a great pity. Understand{% endraw %}
{% raw %}me well. Monsieur Ventnor; it is not for myself.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}As for me, I am perfectly satisfied; but you know{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that there are necessities of business. Go to the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}British Consul, He will give you a passport; or,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}without even taking that trouble, come here with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}anyone who knows you—that will be sufficient. I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}only too much regret even this short delay."{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' I went to the Consul's office. It was Easter holi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}days, and all public offices were closed, and he had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}left Cairo ; so had all the other officials on whom I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}called. Here was I, with nearly a thousand pounds{% endraw %}
