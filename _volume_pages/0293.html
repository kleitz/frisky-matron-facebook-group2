{% raw %}* Estelle, you have triumphed; Tossie cannot have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a breach. Tuft is an infant.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}* What is it that you mean ? Enfant terrible, yes.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' No, he is an infant, a real infant.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Ah, you do not know him. He is more large{% endraw %}
{% raw %}than you.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Still he is an infant. By the laws of England{% endraw %}
{% raw %}every man is an infant until he is twenty-one years{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of age. Lord Tuft is an infant.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' It is droll. Has he a bonne ?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' And, as an infant, he is too young for breaches,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}so your Tossie cannot have her case.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' That is exceflent,' said Estelle.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Furthermore, I will give you a revenge much{% endraw %}
{% raw %}more complete. Take supper with me this evening,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I will get Lord Tuft to meet you. We shall have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a supper fit for the Grand-Duchess of Gerolstein.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bring any friend of yours for me to talk to; I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}don't care whom, only she must be as handsome as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}yourself.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' But it is impossible.'{% endraw %}

{% raw %}meet a lord.'{% endraw %}
