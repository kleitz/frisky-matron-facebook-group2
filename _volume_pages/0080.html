{% raw %}78 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}time coming round, and he was beginning to become{% endraw %}
{% raw %}seriously uneasy, when she opened her beautiful{% endraw %}
{% raw %}eyes, gazed at him, and whispered, ' Kiss me.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He stooped his head, and received a long, sweet,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lingering, thrilling, clinging kiss. She then swooned{% endraw %}
{% raw %}once more, but after awhile became herself again,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}but very gradually.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Well,' said she, ' rational dress is a deal better{% endraw %}
{% raw %}than a roll of carpets, any way. I've come to take{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you away on a bike. Your machine is below; I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bought it for you with that money you lost to me at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ecarte. I am good for a ninety-mile run. I will show{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you the way—first to a place I know of where we{% endraw %}
{% raw %}can get a good breakfast, then we shall run to Rheims,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and then vogue la galere.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}She walked out of the house, and sprang on her{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bicycle, to all appearances a remarkably handsome{% endraw %}
{% raw %}young man with a beautiful moustache and a dandi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fied dress. Only the feet were too small. She had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}engulphed her hands in huge buckskin gloves ; but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nothing would persuade her to wear boots too large.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'I got this moustache,' said she, 'when riding in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rural parts of England. Some of the landladies of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}country inns would not admit me without a skirt—I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hate to mix my costumes, so I never carried one.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I just mounted a moustache instead ; and the land¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ladies looked at me sometimes, I can tell you, with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a very great amount of favour indeed. Now for our{% endraw %}
{% raw %}breakfast, then we shall ride to the monkey tope,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and then for Rheims.'{% endraw %}
