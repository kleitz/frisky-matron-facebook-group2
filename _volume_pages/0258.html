{% raw %}256 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' My dear Julia, you are talking of what I know{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nothing about.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Then, you are not now engaged to be married ?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' I am certainly not engaged to be married.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Have you ever been engaged ?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Never!'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Do you mean to tell me that your father and old{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mr. Blogg were not partners in pork, and you and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}young Mr. Blogg were not engaged as children ?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Really, Julia, this is too bad. You terrible girl,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you would make a joke of anything.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' But I assure you, my dear, I am not joking. I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}am really in earnest, and I begin to think this is a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}very serious matter.'{% endraw %}
