{% raw %}16 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Diavaroo. You are my father; you are also my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mother. Now I can go back with my own proper{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wife to Mauritius. I plenty happy man, sahib.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Master ever wanting me, I coming running to master.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Master, when he returning back, I going Calcutta,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bombay, Madras—I coming soon as master calls.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I think I go back with gentleman and lady; my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}own wife go back ayah business on ship. Coming{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mauritius, I begin little sawcar business—sawcar{% endraw %}
{% raw %}business best for pice-making.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A few minutes afterwards the express steamed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from the gare, Mhadhurah's grateful eyes fixed to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the last on his benefactor.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Yussuff for a time was lost in a reverie.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}* I am for the moment happy,' he thought. ' Is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}this good-nature, or is it pure selfishness ? With{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fifty pounds I have made that man perfectly blest,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and I am in consequence gratified myself. Yet I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}know that he will become a sawcar—a usurer. He{% endraw %}
{% raw %}will cause misery to hundreds. His present delight{% endraw %}
{% raw %}makes me happy. Am I, therefore, a good fellow ?{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Honestly, I do not know. Action is the only true{% endraw %}
{% raw %}means of self-knowledge. Let us act.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A few minutes saw Yussuff at the Credit Lyonnais.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He soon found himself handed over to an official,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}whom he took to be an Englishman, so perfect was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his accent. After a short explanation, this gentle¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}man, called De Tenaille by his colleagues, ushered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Yussuff into an inner and most sumptuous apart¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ment, where a very great man indeed sat in state.{% endraw %}
