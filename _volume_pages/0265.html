{% raw %}DON JUAN 263{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mrs. Bellairs stood up and began to copy, standing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in front of Mr. Simkin watching his feet. She leaned{% endraw %}
{% raw %}slightly forward, pressing back her skirts, while her{% endraw %}
{% raw %}little feet ' peeped in and out as though they feared{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the light.' She soon caught the idea. Simkin waltzed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}towards her, and his arm glided round her waist.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}She raised her hand to his shoulder, and off they{% endraw %}
{% raw %}went round the room. It was somehow quite dif¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ferent from the same thing in a crowded ball-room.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He glanced up and met her eyes, high above him,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fixed on his. She raised her lower eyelids until her{% endraw %}
{% raw %}orbs were veiled, while her colour was heightened{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and glowing.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Stop, Mr. Simkin,' said she, sitting down, while{% endraw %}
{% raw %}her chest heaved. ' What would they think if any¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}body should see us ? I hope the people across the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}way cannot see through the blind, and indeed I don't{% endraw %}
{% raw %}think they can, for I tried to look in this morning{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from the top of an omnibus, and I could not.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'Ah, Mrs. Bellairs,' said Simkin, 'you must{% endraw %}
{% raw %}promise me quite a lot of dances during the rest of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the season.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' I shall be only too glad, Mr. Simkin, but we must{% endraw %}
{% raw %}remember Mrs. Grundy. You know my unfortunate{% endraw %}
{% raw %}position, married as I am to a man who left me a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}week after our wedding. You understand how par¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ticular I must be, and how utterly and completely{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I rely on you. I must confess that I love waltzing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with you, but we must be cautious. I should dearly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}like to have you as a friend. Will you be one ?'{% endraw %}
