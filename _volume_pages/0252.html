{% raw %}250 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Miss Browne stopped to look at a facsimile of a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fresco on the Deria Doulet palace at Seringapatam,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}representing the surprise of an English division by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Tippoo Sahib. The English commander was repre¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sented as sitting in a palankeen, looking very grave,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with his finger in his mouth. A tumbrel was blow¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing up, and a bheestie water-carrier was advancing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with some water in a bucket to arrest the explosion.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The English, in square, were represented as fight¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing bravely, and dying manfully, surrounded by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thousands of Tippoo's troops.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}When Miss Browne turned, she had lost her party,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which was headed by Mrs. Bellairs. She proceeded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to search for them, and at last came upon Mrs.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bellairs, whom she found alone, she having been{% endraw %}
{% raw %}just left by some gentleman whom, in the hurry. Miss{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Browne did not recognise. In fact, she only saw the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}back of his head, and noticed that he had gray hair.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mrs. Bellairs then proposed that they should go{% endraw %}
{% raw %}out by the West Kensington entrance (which was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}now nearer than that by which they had come into{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the structure), and train home. Miss Browne{% endraw %}
{% raw %}agreed, and they proceeded in the desired direction,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}when, as they were beginning to ascend the hill,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mrs. Bellairs said :{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Stop for one minute, and have a look at the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}scene. You know, Kitty, how prosaic and sad-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}minded we English are supposed to be, and yet I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}think the palaces of Aladdin had not a prettier scene{% endraw %}
{% raw %}than that before us. Let us sit down and admire.'{% endraw %}
