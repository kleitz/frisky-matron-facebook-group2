{% raw %}2IO A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}French business, or a case which might lie in both{% endraw %}
{% raw %}countries.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'But,' said the chief, 'you should know French,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and be able to make your way about.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Jim told him that he had been born in Paris, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lived there until the age of twelve, and that more{% endraw %}
{% raw %}recently, when eighteen, he had gone there as groom{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to a sporting gentleman, who had come to grief,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}leaving Jim to shift for himself, which he did by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}filling in rapid succession about twenty different places{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in as many different parts of the city. This fictitious{% endraw %}
{% raw %}account he accompanied by such a whirlwind of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}names of faubourgs, quarters, boulevards, squares,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}streets, alleys, cafes, casinos, kiosks, and accounts{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the customs of the country, that the chief got{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bewildered.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Well,' said he, ' it so happens that there is a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Frenchman here who has asked me to take up a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}case which began in Whitechapel, and will probably{% endraw %}
{% raw %}end in France. I'fl have him in.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Frenchman entered and stated his case.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Jim took up the running, and, commencing with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}one place in Paris mentioned by the Frenchman,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}described every street of any consequence within a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quarter of a mile of it. He then digressed, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}began relating his own apocryphal experiences, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the places where he had lived all over the city.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' It is extraordinaire,' said monsieur. ' I am{% endraw %}
{% raw %}myself a Parisian, and here is this jentlemans who{% endraw %}
{% raw %}knows of Paris more than I do.'{% endraw %}
