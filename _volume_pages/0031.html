{% raw %}BOHEMIA 29{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Nothing loath to show off, Porthos placed two{% endraw %}
{% raw %}small tables, three feet apart, with a small pile of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}books on each, and laid a cane across, about three{% endraw %}
{% raw %}feet six inches from the ground, and with a mighty{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bound cleared it. The house shook as he reached{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the floor.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Yussuff jumped the stick without an effort, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}alighted on the points of his toes as light as a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thistle-down, or a pole-jumper. His descent was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}noiseless,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Porthos immediately saw that he had encountered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a trained athlete. He propped up the cane to a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}height of four feet three inches and cleared itâ€”{% endraw %}
{% raw %}almost, but not quite. He touched it, but the graze{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was so slight that the cane was not displaced,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Yussuff cleared it with ease.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Blank astonishment fell on the three musketeers.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Now,' said Yussuff, ' I challenge you to jump up{% endraw %}
{% raw %}on the mantelpiece, and stand there.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The chimneypiece was about four feet eight inches{% endraw %}
{% raw %}high and two feet and a half broad. Porthos looked{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at it and shook his head.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}* It is impossible,' said he. ' It cannot be done.'{% endraw %}

{% raw %}that mantelpiece without a run.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Done, for a pony !' said Yussuff.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}a feather on the mantel. He then turned and faced{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the room, and with one bound alighted on the table.{% endraw %}
