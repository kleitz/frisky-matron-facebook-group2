{% raw %}PARIS 13{% endraw %}
{% raw %}reminiscences fill the many corridors of my brain{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with mighty feelings, as if too much for your most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}humble of petitioners, I am in constant series of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}aspirations perennial, and not by any means discon¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tinuous, that the Supreme Rulers endow you with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the mens sana in corpore sano, medicinally, and of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spirit which conduces to a character worthy of admi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ration. I confide to your discrimination an article{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which my strong vanity designs for publication. It{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is an attempt, but in a modulated degree, to assert{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the future coming greatness of our race. With our{% endraw %}
{% raw %}subtle intellectual keenness, and the solids of British{% endraw %}
{% raw %}learning, to which I am now trusting to add some{% endraw %}
{% raw %}French polish, our castes must reach fame in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}futurity, I have been observant in my peregrina¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tions, and to succeed now it is necessary to assimi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}late the most new that there is of all that is about{% endraw %}
{% raw %}us—to be up to date. You will observe that my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}article is type-written. This will please the editor.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}This missive will be despatched to you from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}light-headed city of Paris. From there I shall take{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a short trip, or more than one, as the pursuit of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}knowledge will require, and in about a month I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shall take my way, by the aid of the puffing monster,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the Eiffel country, and meet once more you, my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}good friend of the past. Till that happy hour, I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bid you adieu, with bahallah salaam.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Yours ever truly,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Yussuff Khan Bahadoor.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' P.S.—Write to me to paste restante in Paris.'{% endraw %}
