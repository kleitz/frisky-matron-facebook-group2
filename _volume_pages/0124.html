{% raw %}122 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' And you brought ?' said Estelle.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' A bottle of my own,' said Porthos, who was half-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}seas over, ' and here it is. For glass I have a silver{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cigarette-case.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' And I a pocket-pistol,' said another,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Yussuff rapidly explained that Estelle could shoot{% endraw %}
{% raw %}it down his throat from on high, as we often see{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Hindoos of different castes assist each other. They{% endraw %}
{% raw %}were all ready.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Then Estelle sang her song; no parody this time.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It was weird, that Marseillaise, sung over sleeping{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Paris—over that city which had so often quivered to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}its sound. A feeling of awe overcame them all as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they listened on their knees. Would Paris hear it ?{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A twelve-pounder fired where they were would{% endraw %}
{% raw %}scarcely be heard below. Estelle herself was im¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pressed. She was impulsive, but she loved to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}believe in the depths of her own feelings. It was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a scene to be ever remembered as, inspired by the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}concentrated essence of the feelings of that mighty{% endraw %}
{% raw %}city focussed on that tiny platform at an elevation{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of one thousand feet, she burst forth into an{% endraw %}
{% raw %}inspired patriotic address, in which, fired with the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}courage, and the greatness, and the glory of France,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}she exhorted them to remember always to die for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the ' Patrie.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}She swayed their feelings; at that moment her{% endraw %}
{% raw %}soul mastered all the other souls. There was one{% endraw %}
{% raw %}electrical shout, one lingering glance of love and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pride over the sleeping city. They entered the lift.{% endraw %}
