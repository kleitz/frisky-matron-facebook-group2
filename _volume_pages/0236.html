{% raw %}234 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Browne knew absolutely nothing about him, except{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that he was six feet two, moderately well dressed,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and had the manners of a gentleman. She knew{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nothing about what he really was. Since then the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}proposal of a friendship, hinted at by her, was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}eagerly grasped by him, and she, claiming American{% endraw %}
{% raw %}prerogatives, had gone on many a voyage of dis¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}covery under his escort. This gave them ample{% endraw %}
{% raw %}opportunities of getting to know each other{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thoroughly, and, in fact, this is one of the chief{% endraw %}
{% raw %}advantages of the American system of dispensing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with chaperons. It often happens that a man is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cowed by the presence of a chaperon, while in those{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cases where the guardian dragon is herself a frisky{% endraw %}
{% raw %}matron, she may attract to herself those attentions{% endraw %}
{% raw %}due to the spinster, or she may cause young Benedict{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to say to himself: 'I say! if this be matrimony—if{% endraw %}
{% raw %}this is the way a wife of mine would go on—I had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}better think twice before crossing the Rubicon.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}They set about a task perhaps never attempted{% endraw %}
{% raw %}before, and that is, a complete study of London.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}This subject had one great advantage in Caldwyn's{% endraw %}
{% raw %}eyes : it would never end. New York can be known{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in a week. All worth seeing in it lies within twenty{% endraw %}
{% raw %}minutes' drive of Stuart's Marble Palace. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sights of Liverpool, of Birmingham, can be seen in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a day. In most cities the gems of a place are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}collected in one small quarter, but London is an{% endraw %}
{% raw %}immense agglomeration of huge cities, each one ot{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which, in itself, takes a long time to know.{% endraw %}
