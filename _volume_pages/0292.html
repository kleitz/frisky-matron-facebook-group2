{% raw %}290 A FRISKY MATRON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}* That is a difficult matter, Estelle.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Not at all difficult; I wfll propose to you, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you will marry me. Behold, I kneel. You, the most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}noble, more handsome than Athos, more spirituel{% endraw %}
{% raw %}than Aramis. Oh ! if I do not marry a Mflord, I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shall want my destiny.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Estelle, you are rapidly learning English.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Yes ; it was for you.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Have your fellow fairies taught you the meaning{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the phrase, " Don't be silly "?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' But, yes.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'Then get up ; your nose is getting red.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Estelle, like a good girl, did as she was bid.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' But you will help me ?' said she.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Why, certainly. She has caught her heir, but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}will she cook him ? There is many a slip.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' So I told her, and she said in that case she{% endraw %}
{% raw %}would bring a breach, and that would be quite as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}good. Now, promise to marry me. Do not do so.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I wifl bring a breach. That wifl be a pair of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}breaches; in fine, I wifl be revenged on her.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Estelle, remember we are in sober, sensible,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}staid, moral England, where we all live by rule.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}What is this Milord's name ?'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Milord Tuft.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' Tuft ? Know him well; a nice boy. Estelle, I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}can help you. Excuse me a moment.'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He walked to a table, took a much-gilded volume{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and turned the leaves rapidly.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}' That makes him not yet twenty,' said he.{% endraw %}
